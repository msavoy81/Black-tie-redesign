Our Story

A Legacy Worth Honoring

Black Tie was built around the memory of a family patriarch whose values of hard work, generosity, and dapper style still shape how we treat every client who walks through the door.

We've paired that old-school barbershop spirit with the comforts of a modern spa: licensed, highly skilled hands, premium products, and a space designed for real relaxation, not just a quick trim.
